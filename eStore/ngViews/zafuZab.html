zafu

our comfortable and supportive zafus are filled with buckwheat hulls and covered in hemp fabrics with beautiful patterns and colors created with low-impact dyes.

use. buckwheat hull filling allows the sitter to shift the filling for a very personal fit and will retain its position.

to further accommodate the sitter's needs, it is possible to unzip the cover to remove or add filling to make slight or dramatic adjustments in height.

Buckwheat hulls are very slow to break down. These meditation pillows can be used for years without significant changes.

Sewn-in strap for easy transport

CARE: Unzip cover to pour out buckwheat hull filling to gently wash and air dry cover.
