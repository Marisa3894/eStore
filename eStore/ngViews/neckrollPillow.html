use. our pillows SUPPORT the neck, rather than suspend it as most pillows do. This unfailing support provides relief of neck and back pain, headaches and... from countless testimonials, snoring.

the advantage of the pre-flattened buckwheat hull filling is that it maintains its volume, not compressing during the night. This allows the neck muscles to truly relax, correct alignment and continued relaxation throughout the the shoulders on down the frame.

turn or shift the pillows in any way to get the maximum height or cushion. Use them by themselves or with your traditional pillows, but always place the buckwheat hull on top.

contruction. the Buckwheat Hull NECKROLL and 2 PILLOW SIZES - 11"x17" and 14"x20" are designed with the greatest attention to detail. Locally grown, sewn and filled in the United States.

20 years of experience has proven our standard three shapes/ sizes will accommodate a wide variety of body types and sleeping positions.

The pillow case is organic cotton drill with a zipper to remove or add filling for the utmost tailored fit. This is an indispensable feature for adjusting.

The pillows are filled with organically grown, triple cleaned, pre-flattened buckwheat hulls. The buckwheat hulls take years to break down. With little to no residue, they are great for people with allergies.

The all-natural materials allow air to circulate, staying cool and fresh.
